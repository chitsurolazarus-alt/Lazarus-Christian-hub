About Lazarus's Christian Hub

Learn more about this AI-powered Bible study tool and how to use it.

Our Mission

Lazarus's Christian Hub is designed to help believers and seekers alike engage with the Bible through modern technology. Our AI-powered features make Bible study interactive, accessible, and meaningful for the digital age.

AI-Powered Features

  • Bible Quiz Generator: Creates personalized multiple-choice quizzes based on your selected categories and difficulty.
  • Scripture Search: Quickly find verses using natural language or references.
  • Daily Motivation: Receive Bible verses tailored to your current emotional and spiritual needs.
  • Psalms Prayers: Access prayers from the Book of Psalms categorized by life situations.

Technology

This application is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, using a simulated AI engine to generate content. All Bible verses are from the New International Version (NIV).

How to Use

  1. Navigate between sections using the sidebar menu.
  2. Generate Bible quizzes by selecting your preferences.
  3. Search scriptures by keyword, topic, or reference.
  4. Get daily motivation by selecting your current need.
  5. Explore Psalms prayers by category.

Share & Contribute

This project is open source and hosted on GitHub. Feel free to contribute, suggest features, or report issues.
